Useful Insects/Arachnids


Ladybugs
Bees
Spiders
  • Ladybugs help get rid of plant-feeding insects.Ladybugs mostly eat Aphids, even the Ladybug larvae eats Aphids. Ladybugs are not harmful to your plants or you at all.
    Bees help flowers grow by pollinating it. Bees also obtain honey from the flowers which we can use for food. Bees pollinate one-sixth of the world's flowering plant species.
    Spiders can help get rid of all kinds of insects in your house or in your garden. The silk of the spiders come in lots of use for parachutes and bullet-proof vests. Most Spiders are not harmful to humans.

    These Insects can help keep crops safe and healthy.
    Most of these Insects are common throughout the world!
