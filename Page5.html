Dangerous Insects/Arachnids

Fire Ants

Fire Ants are agrressive and will attack if disturbed. The bite/sting of a Fire Ant injects a toxic venom that feels like fire. Fire Ants can overpower almost anything because they go in swarms.These Ants can make a raft (made by themselves) to go over water.

Black Widow Spider

A female Black Widow Spider is considered the most venomous spider in North America. Its venom was said to be 15 times as toxic as the venom of the Prairie Rattlesnake. Luckily for us spiders are nonaggressive and only bites if threatened.

Giant Asian Hornet

This hornet is known to be the largest hornet in the world. Not only is it big but its also extremely dangerous, its sting can damage the tissues. The sting of the Giant Asian Hornet was said to feel "like a hot nail being driven into my leg".
