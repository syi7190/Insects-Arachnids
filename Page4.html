  • The Horse Fly was known to be the fastest flying insect in the world. This fly can fly up to 90mph and can control its flying very well. The Female Horse Flies actually bites animals to obtain blood.
    This type of spider is actually able to make a sound of a whistle. The spider does this be rubbing its legs together. This tarantula is also known as the Barking Spider or the Bird-eating Spider.
    This unique beetle is endangered which means if you find one in the Frigate Island, keep it safe. This type of beetle is the largest of the tenebrionid beetles and the most at risk.
